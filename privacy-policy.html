Generally, residents of California have the following rights regarding their personal data:

  • Right to Opt-Out
    Under CCPA, every California resident has the right to request that a business stops selling their personal information to third parties.
  • Rhight to Deletion
    You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information collected by the business, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination
    You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your CCPA rights

Rhights of EEU residents

We handle and respond to your requests without undue delay and within one month of receiving the request, unless the request is particularly complex, in which case, our response time may be extended to a maximum of three months as allowed by Article 12 of the GDPR.

  • Rhight to Access
    You have the right to obtain confirmation as to whether personal data concerning you is being processed and, if so, access to that personal data. Please be aware that access may be limited due to considerations related to intellectual property or trade secrets.
  • Right to Rectification
    You have the right to rectify inaccurate personal data concerning you and to have incomplete personal data completed.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
    You have the right to request the erasure of personal data concerning you, subject to certain conditions.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing
    You have the right to request the restriction of processing of your personal data in certain circumstances. If you have the right to restriction, we will only process your data with your consent, for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims, or to protect individuals or important public interests.
  • Right to Data Portability
    You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and have the right to transmit that data to another controller if the processing is based on consent or contract performance.
  • Right to Object
    You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data (including for direct marketing purposes) that is based on our legitimate interests, at any time. We will cease processing your personal data unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate reasons for the processing that outweigh your interests, rights, and freedoms, or if it is necessary for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent
    If we process your personal data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.
